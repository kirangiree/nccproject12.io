Nepal

Nepal is very beautiful tourist destination in the word.This place is a god gifted place.Because this small country is packed full of Nature,Traditions,Cultures,Religions and so on.Tourists can experience the hot Terai climate to a very freezing cold Himalayan climate.In the list of top 10 mountains in the world 8 of them are from Nepal.Tourists can spend a very good holidays in Nepal.They can feel the nature from very close in Nepal.Tourists can feel change in temperature in the journey of every 1 hour in Nepal because in Nepal there is a deserted Manang and Mustang to Freezing Ice Mt.Everest.The himalayas of Nepal is also known as third Pole of the World.Nepal is full of green mountains,beautiful rivers,waterfalls,lakes and so on.

Beside Natural beauty Nepal is also very popular for the unique traditions and cultures.The main cities of Nepal are full of traditional,cultural and religious monuments.There are 125 ethnic group and 123 languages.So,Tourists can enjoy the native culture in Nepal.Nepal is famous for various Traditional arts,Music and literature.The major religion in Nepal is Hinduism, and the Pashupatinath Temple, the world's largest temple of Shiva, located in Kathmandu, attracts many pilgrims and tourists. Other Hindu pilgrimage sites include the temple complex in Swargadwari in the Pyuthan district; Janaki Mandir in Janakpurdham in Mithila region; Lake Gosainkunda near Dhunche; the temples at Devghat; Manakamana temple in the Gorkha District; Pathibhara near Phungling; and Mahamrityunjaya Shivasan Nepal in Palpa District where the biggest metallic idol of Lord Shiva is located.Buddhism is the largest minority religion. The World Heritage site at Lumbini, which is traditionally considered to be the birthplace of Gautama Buddha, is an important pilgrimage site. Another prominent Buddhist site is Swayambhunath, the Monkey Temple, in Kathmandu.
